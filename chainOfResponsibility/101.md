Chain of Responsibility is one of the most useful patterns in Go because **HTTP middleware is basically Chain of Responsibility**.

The idea is:

> **Pass a request through a chain of handlers until one handles it or the chain ends.**

---

Suppose someone enters your website.

# Difference From Strategy

Strategy:

```text
Choose ONE algorithm.
```

```text
PaymentStrategy
```

Chain:

```text
Run MANY handlers sequentially.
```

---

# Difference From Decorator

Decorator:

```text
Wrap object and add behavior.
```

Chain:

```text
Pass request through many handlers.
```

Although they look very similar structurally.
